Take a walk through time …

England’s Jurassic Coast reveals 185 million years of geological and evolutionary history in just 95 miles!

A Natural World Heritage Site, it covers the southern coast from East Devon to Dorset, where changing geology and erosion has created an environment of great diversity and beauty, offering key insight into the Earth’s development and the evolution of life.

The rocks and fossils tell a fascinating story of ancient deserts, tropical seas and dinosaur infested swamps.

Explore The Jurassic Coast’s unique features by panning through painstakingly assembled offshore photography covering the length of the coast. Other resources include animations which explain geological sequences and plentiful expert notes.

Developed by Quantedit, Jurassic is a free app, available on the iPhone App Store.
